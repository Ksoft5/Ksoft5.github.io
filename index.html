Kendrick Hardaway

Environmental & Ecological Engineering Doctoral Candidate

Climate adaptation | Infrastructure planning | Emerging Systems | Ecological Economics

I study climate adaptation and industrial ecology. My research is focused on the adaptation of infrastructure systems and the environmental impacts of emerging technologies. I identify missing feedback loops and interactions using simulation, modeling, statistics, system dynamics, and risk science.

News

My research is at the intersection of:

  • Urban and civil infrastructure systems
  • Climate adaptation and uncertainty
  • Ecological engineering and economics
